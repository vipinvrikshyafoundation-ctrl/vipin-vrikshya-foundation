About Us

Our Vision

To create a greener and more sustainable future by promoting environmental conservation, tree plantation, and community welfare. We envision a world where every individual contributes to preserving nature for future generations.

Our Mission

To actively engage communities in environmental conservation through tree plantation drives, awareness campaigns, and social welfare activities. We are committed to making a positive impact on the environment and society through dedicated action and community participation.

Legal Status

Vipin Vrikshya Foundation is a registered non-profit organization under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013. We operate with complete transparency and accountability in all our initiatives.

Meet Our Founder

Founder

Lalit Sangwan

Founder & Visionary

"Every tree we plant today is a breath of fresh air for tomorrow. Together, we can restore the green cover that our planet desperately needs."

Lalit Sangwan founded Vipin Vrikshya Foundation with a deep commitment to environmental conservation and a vision for a greener future. His passion for nature and concern for the rapidly deteriorating environment drove him to take concrete action.

Witnessing the alarming rate of deforestation, increasing pollution levels, and the devastating impact on wildlife habitats, Lalit decided to be the change he wanted to see. Through Vipin Vrikshya Foundation, he channels his dedication into meaningful action, inspiring communities to join the green revolution.

Our Story

The Journey That Started With A Vision

The Problem We Saw

In today's rapidly developing world, we witnessed a heartbreaking reality: forests are being cleared at an alarming rate for townships and industrial expansion. Carbon emissions are skyrocketing, pollution levels are reaching dangerous heights, and bird habitats are disappearing before our eyes.

The very lungs of our planet‚Äîour trees‚Äîare being sacrificed for short-term gains, leaving future generations to face the consequences of global warming and environmental degradation.

The Realization

We realized that to combat pollution, global warming, and habitat destruction, we need to bring back the green cover around us. Plants are not just decorative elements‚Äîthey are essential for our survival, providing oxygen, absorbing carbon dioxide, offering shelter to countless species, and maintaining ecological balance.

The solution was clear: we needed to act now, not tomorrow. Every tree planted today is a step toward healing our planet.

Taking Action

This realization sparked the birth of Vipin Vrikshya Foundation in April 2025. We didn't just want to talk about environmental issues‚Äîwe wanted to create tangible change. We started organizing plantation drives, engaging communities, and inspiring people to take responsibility for the environment.

From our first sapling to over 400 trees planted today, every step has been driven by passion, commitment, and the belief that together, we can make a difference.

Growing Together

But our mission goes beyond just planting trees. We believe in holistic community service. That's why we also organize winter cloth distribution drives to help underprivileged individuals, because caring for people and planet go hand in hand.

Today, Vipin Vrikshya Foundation stands as a testament to what dedicated individuals can achieve when they unite for a common cause‚Äîa greener, healthier, and more sustainable future for all.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers!

How can I become a member of Vipin Vrikshya Foundation?

Simply fill out our membership registration form above. We offer two membership options: Free membership and Premium membership (‚Çπ1,500) which includes a branded t-shirt, ID card, and exclusive merchandise.

Are donations tax-deductible?

As a registered Section 8 company, we are working towards obtaining 80G certification. Donation receipts will be provided for all contributions. Please contact us for specific tax benefit queries.

How often do you organize plantation drives?

We organize plantation drives 3 to 4 times a year during the most favorable seasons. Our plantation drives typically take place in February, March, July, August, September, and October when weather conditions are ideal for sapling growth and survival. Members receive advance notifications about upcoming events via email and WhatsApp.

Can I volunteer without becoming a member?

Yes! We welcome all volunteers. However, becoming a member (even free membership) helps us keep you updated about all our activities and provides you with official recognition for your contributions.

Where do the plantation drives take place?

We organize plantation drives at various locations including public parks, schools, community spaces, and designated green areas in Delhi NCR. Specific locations are shared with members before each drive.

How are donations utilized?

Donations are used for purchasing saplings, plantation tools, maintenance of planted trees, organizing community welfare activities like cloth distribution, and conducting environmental awareness campaigns. We maintain complete transparency in fund utilization.
